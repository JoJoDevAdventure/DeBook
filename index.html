What is DEBOOK?

A book is a book, and DEBOOK is also...

Debook is also a

marketplace

DEBOOK is the place to buy, sell and exchange debooks, a new version of books that are fairer to authors and readers in a very user friendly experience. Buy, sell or rent your favorite books.

Debook is also a

social platform

Each debook collection has its own community. Authors and readers are creators inside those communities. Your debook is your passport to a community full of content around the book you love.

Debook is also a

web3 publisher

Each debook is a tokenized digital collectible. This gives you complete ownership, and gives many benefits to the author, such as control over their book's community and content.

Our purpose is to make books fairer for authors and readers, bring people together, and innovate how much value you get from a book.
Learn more in our MANIFIESTO.

The differences between
DEBOOK and 'debook'

DEBOOK is the platform, Marketplace and tokenization tool. DEBOOK is the term we use to refer to the brand. The future of books.

"debook” is the new book’s format, a book that you can use to read, watch, interact with, own and exchange. A digital collectible that grants you access to a private community around that book. A new version of books.
